Rational Elliptic Function

Given a normalized low-pass tolerance scheme — passband gain Gp, stopband gain Ga, and stopband edge ωAN — the moduli k1 and k2 are obtained and the elliptic rational function RN(w) of minimal order N is constructed from its zeros and poles.
